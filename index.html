Abby loves to draw. Drawing is her favorite! She uses her colored pencils to draw grass, trees, birds, children, parents, babies, and everything around her. As a doll with a plastic mouth, art is how she best expresses herself.

Meet Sarah

Sarah loves the outdoors. Sometimes she comes off as shy. In nature is where she feels she can truly be herself. Bring Sarah to play with in a forest and see her come to life! Her spirit is especially strong at night.
